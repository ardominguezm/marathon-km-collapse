\section*{Data availability}

The split-time records analyzed in this work are publicly
available from the official results pages of the four World
Marathon Majors: the Boston Athletic Association
(\url{https://www.baa.org/races/boston-marathon/results}),
the Chicago Marathon
(\url{https://www.chicagomarathon.com/runners/results/}),
New York Road Runners
(\url{https://results.nyrr.org/}), and the Berlin Marathon
(\url{https://www.bmw-berlin-marathon.com/en/facts-and-figures/results-archive/}).
The full analysis pipeline---including data cleaning,
Kramers--Moyal estimation, Langevin simulations, and
figure generation---is available as an open-source repository
at \url{https://github.com/andydominguez/marathon-km-collapse},
which contains the annotated Jupyter notebooks required to
reproduce all results and figures reported in this paper.
